# ArtTech — AI × Art × Physical Space

Exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence, generative art, and physical installations. Inspired by [teamLab](https://www.teamlab.art/) and the emerging field of autonomous, reactive art that lives in the physical world.

## Vision

Create physical art installations that:
- **Sense** — perceive their environment (people, movement, sound, weather, time)
- **Think** — use AI to generate, adapt, evolve artistic responses in real-time
- **Express** — manifest through light, sound, projection, kinetic sculpture, haptics
- **Connect** — create shared experiences where humans become part of the artwork

## Inspiration

- **teamLab** — Forest of Autonomous Resonating Life, Borderless, Planets
- **Random International** — Rain Room, Swarm Study
- **Studio Drift** — Franchise Freedom (drone swarms as art)
- **Refik Anadol** — AI-driven data sculptures, Living Architecture
- **Olafur Eliasson** — Weather Project, environmental perception art

## What Makes This Different

teamLab uses motion sensors + pre-coded reactions. The next evolution:
- **Generative AI** creates the art in real-time (not pre-animated)
- **LLMs** give installations personality, memory, narrative
- **Computer vision** enables deeper understanding of human behavior
- **Multi-modal AI** responds to voice, gesture, emotion, crowd dynamics
- **Autonomous agents** — each element has its own behavior, emergent collective patterns
